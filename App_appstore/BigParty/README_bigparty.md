# Big party - AR Game

## ü¶ÑFeeding adorable little animals in augmented reality. 

## Description
 <p>Augmented reality technologies will allow your child to feed adorable pets anywhere.  </p>

## Capabilities

Available in the app:
- 10 color schemes
- 11 languages switchable directly in the app
- 6 3D models of pets
- 17 3D models of treats for pets
- 2 types of instructions for each game (standard instructions and instructions in augmented reality)
- the application will save the best result of the game and the last result of the game

There are 3 game options available in the app:
- Game "Cats and Dogs":
    - two types of pets and three types of treats will appear in the surrounding space 
    - throw treats to pets and if you pick up the right treat, the pet will disappear with a beautiful effect
    - if you throw the wrong treat to the pet, then you will hear the sound of a blow and the pet will not disappear
    - game time is fixed
- Game "All friends":
    - six types of pets and 17 types of treats will appear in the space around you
    - throw treats to pets and if you pick up the right treat, the pet will disappear with a beautiful effect
    - if you throw the wrong treat to the pet, then you will hear the sound of a blow and the pet will not disappear
    - game time is fixed
- Game "Dessert Time":
    - only unicorns and sweets will appear in the space around you
    - throw sweets to unicorns and they will disappear with a beautiful effect
    - is the simplest version of the game
    - game time is fixed
